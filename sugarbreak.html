SugarBreak — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

SugarBreak ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

SugarBreak collects the following data to provide personalized coaching and sugar tracking:

Health and fitness information including symptoms you select during onboarding, daily check-in data (mood, craving intensity, sugar-free status), and notes you write about your day. Food scan history when you use the sugar scanner feature. Photos only when you choose to use the photo scanner to identify food items. App activity including your interactions with the coaching feature, motivation vault answers, and progress data. Schedule information including wake time, sleep time, work hours, and lunch time that you provide during setup.

How We Use Your Data

All data is used solely to provide app functionality and personalize your experience. Specifically, we use your data to generate personalized daily coaching briefings, provide AI-powered craving support through MyCoach, predict craving patterns and send timely alerts, track your progress and streaks, and identify food sugar content through the scanner.

AI Processing

SugarBreak uses Google's Gemini AI to generate personalized coaching responses. Your check-in history, craving logs, and motivation vault answers are sent to the Gemini API to generate relevant, personalized advice. Google processes this data according to their AI API terms of service. We do not use your data to train AI models.

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using the app's local storage. We do not maintain servers or databases that store your personal information. Your data never leaves your device except when sent to the Gemini AI API for real-time coaching responses, and to the Open Food Facts API for food product lookups (no personal data is sent for food searches).

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than providing app functionality. We do not display ads. We do not use analytics tracking services.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by going to your device Settings, then Apps, then SugarBreak, then Storage, then Clear Data. This permanently removes all locally stored data. Since we do not store data on external servers, clearing app data results in complete deletion.

Children's Privacy

SugarBreak is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Subscriptions

SugarBreak offers optional in-app subscriptions processed through Google Play. Payment information is handled entirely by Google Play and is never accessed by us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
